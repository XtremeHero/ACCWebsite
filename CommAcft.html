Commercial Aircraft

Commercial airplanes are one the biggest industries in the world and the driving force of getting people from all walks of life from point A to B safely and timely.

ACC is the number one leader in manufacturing reliable and safe commercial airplanes. ACC receives contracts from thousands of private and commercial organizations including Boeing, Delta, Southwest, Alaska, Spirit, and many others.

Every airplane goes through a 572 point inspection and bears the renowned ACC "gold seal" of approval for workmanship.

Unlike other organizations who cover just one year or less after delivery we are the only manufacturer who guarantees our work for five years and provided direct 24/7 customer and tech support for the first year on all our services.

To keep things manageable and ensure quality work, ACC only accepts 8,000 commercial contracts a year; out of those, ACC only builds a set number per model and always leaves 500 spots open for custom builds. See the chart below for the remaining openings for FY21.
