Defensive Technology

We take great pride and honor to serve those who have and continue to sacrifice so much for us. ACC's largest customer base is defense contracts from all branches of the United States military.

The DOD alone drives over 40% of ACC's manufacturing workforce and, when necessary, can request 100% for a limited duration not to exceed one year.

By 2025 ACC will have two additional manufacturing plants to increase production for military aircraft by 50%, helping to keep America and its allies safe.

To keep things manageable and ensure quality work, ACC only accepts 10,000 defense contracts a year; see the chart below for the remaining openings for FY21.
