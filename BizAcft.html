Business Aircraft

Business-class aircraft or more commonly referred to as private jets is one of ACC's premier stables. With the majority of contracts coming from celebrities and elite clientele.

The G6 Gulfstream is one of our most requested builds, thanks to the popular song "G6" by rising artist Dev.

However, unlike commercial aircraft, we build our private jets in one of four sizes; light, mid, super-mid, and large. Manufacturing this way allows us to be more flexible as most of the builds we get in this segment are custom, not necessarily fleet quantities.

To keep things manageable and ensure quality work, ACC only accepts 3,000 business/private jet contracts a year; out of those, ACC only builds a set number per size and always leaves 1500 spots open for custom builds. See the chart below for the remaining openings for FY21.
